About MEWA Regulatory Sandbox

MEWA is launching a Regulatory Sandbox to support innovative technologies in the critical sectors of Environment, Water, and Agriculture.

The Regulatory Sandbox will provide a controlled environment to test, iterate, and prove new technologies with direct regulatory oversight, simplifying the path to deployment.

By participating in MEWA's sandbox, firms can tackle regulatory challenges head-on and break down barriers to innovation.

Objectives of the Regulatory Sandbox

Address regulatory barriers impeding innovation via targeted rule change in the legal framework

Provide regulatory guidance to facilitate compliance of innovation with the existing legal framework

Eligibility

Startups and firms innovating in the environment, water, or agriculture sectors are encouraged to apply

Innovation faces uncertainty about compliance with Saudi Arabia’s existing legislative framework

Innovation faces regulatory barriers or challenges that prevent deployment or scale-up in Saudi Arabia
